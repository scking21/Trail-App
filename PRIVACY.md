# Blackrow Trails — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-17_

Blackrow Trails is built to be private by default. Your location and your planning
data stay on your device. We do not run servers that collect your data, we do
not use analytics or advertising trackers, and we never sell or share your
information.

## What the app stores, and where

All of the following is stored **only on your device** (in the app's local
storage) and is never uploaded to us or anyone else:

- Your trip plan (trails you've added) and Trip Kit selections
- Waypoints you mark
- Tracks you record (the GPS path you walked, with elevations and timestamps)
- Trails you save from a recording
- Your map-mode and gear-list preferences

You can remove any single item in the app, or erase **everything** at once with
**Planner → Privacy & data → "Delete all my data on this device."**

## Location data

- By default, the app requests location permission **only while you are using
  it** ("when in use").
- If you turn on **"Keep recording with the screen off"** before recording a
  track, the app will ask for **"Always" / background location** so it can keep
  recording your track while the app is in the background or the screen is off.
  This is entirely optional, **off by default**, and used **only** while you are
  actively recording. You can revoke it at any time in your device settings.
- In all cases your live GPS position is used **on your device** to center the
  map, record a track, warn you when you stray off a followed route, point the AR
  marker, and place waypoints. **Your GPS position is never transmitted to any
  server.**

## Network requests (what does leave the device)

To show live maps and conditions, the app fetches data for **the area you are
viewing or the point you tap** from third-party providers. These requests reveal
*what you are looking at* (a map region or a tapped coordinate), but they do not
include your device's GPS position:

- **Map tiles:** OpenTopoMap, and AWS Terrarium elevation tiles (for slope
  shading)
- **Public land / parcels:** USGS PAD-US and county/state parcel services (Esri/ArcGIS)
- **Trails & features:** USGS National Map, OpenStreetMap/Overpass
- **Weather:** the U.S. National Weather Service (api.weather.gov) for tap
  forecasts, and RainViewer for the precipitation radar overlay

Each provider handles requests under its own privacy policy. The app sends only
the minimum needed (a bounding box or a single tapped point) and adds no
identifiers.

## Files you export

Backups and GPX exports contain precise coordinates, and recorded-track GPX can
include timestamps (when you were at each point). These files are created only
when **you** choose to export or share them, and they go wherever you send them.
If you'd rather not reveal *when* you were somewhere, enable **"Strip timestamps
from exported track GPX"** in Privacy & data before exporting.

## Permissions

- **Location (when in use)** — center the map, record/follow, AR, waypoints.
- **Location (always / background)** — optional; requested only if you enable
  "Keep recording with the screen off," and used only to continue an active
  track recording in the background.
- **Camera** — only for the AR "point me there" view; no photos are stored or sent.

## Children

Blackrow Trails is a general-audience outdoor app and is not directed at children
under 13. It collects no personal information.
